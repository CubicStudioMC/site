FAQ

If you have a question make sure to head over to the Discord and message a Developer.

What is Cubic Studios?

We are a group of developers, focused on bringing mods, resource packs, and plugins to the Minecraft community.

What are Mods?

Modifications (mods) are Java files that modify Minecraft. They add new Mobs, Structures, Animals, and items. Mods are a great addition to the game.

Can I suggest updates to mods?

Yes you can. Here at Cubic Studios we appreciate feedback from the community. Feel free to message any of the Developers on our Discord.
